About me

I’m Alan Pai, currently a third-year student from National Tsing-Hua University in Taiwan. I’m a Foreign Languages & Literature major coupled with the program of Law.

Humanity science is of my interest since I consider it a door to a bigger world. Hence, I have a habit of keeping myself informed of international news, to know what’s happening around the world. In addition, I have a strong passion for cinema. For me, cinema is not only a kind of entertainment but a sacred form of art and education. It provides me with a mental refuge where I can put all my worries from daily lives aside and immerse in the mesmerizing stories; it also showcases a means of understanding humanity or the world in general, on a more vivid, sympathetic level. I used to want to become a filmmaker in the future, but that strong urge of the wild dream gradually wore thin after I realized how hard the path could be when it comes to striking a good financial balance. Though I'm not studying cinema, humanity science is also something related and complementary; it's vital in shaping my view and cultivating my passion for cinema as well. Hopefully in this way, I can see films from a more diverse angle.


My plan for the future and the present

I plan to go on to further study in the Institute of Law for Science and Technology of NTHU after I graduate. Now I want to focus on honing my language ability and absorbing the knowledge of Law as much as I can. Meanwhile, I’ll keep watching and reviewing movies.

關於我

我叫白祐綸，今年大三，就讀國立清華大學外語系，同時也修讀法律學程。

我對人文社會科學很有興趣，平時也會關注國際新聞，了解世界發生的事情。此外，我對電影有極高度的熱忱，有些人可能只把電影視為一種娛樂，但我認為不只如此，電影是提供我心靈慰藉的重要來源，且它也讓我能以更生動、更具同理心的角度來了解人性、探索世界。雖然曾經的電影夢（導演）已因現實因素而漸漸消退，現在也非學習電影，但不代表目前專攻的人文領域與電影毫不相干。有了此領域的幫助，讓我在欣賞電影時有更多元的媒介。
大學畢業後，我計畫考取清大科法所，繼續專研法律，在那之前，我會持續增進自己的語言能力與法律知識，並維持欣賞、評論電影的興趣。
